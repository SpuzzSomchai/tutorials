1. Show size of folders - "du -h"

2. Remove folders (recursively) - "rm "foldername" -r"

3. Copy files to local harddrive - "adb pull "/source/folder" "/destination/folder""

4. Copy files to phone/sd card - "adb push "/source/folder" "/destination/folder""

5. Change animation scales: 
5a. settings put global window_animation_scale X.X
5b. settings put global transition_animation_scale X.X
5c. settings put global animator_duration_scale X.X
